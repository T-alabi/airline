Requirements

For Flight Attendents

In order to be considered, you must:

  • Be atleast 21 years of age at the time of applying
  • Be eligoble to work in the U.S.
  • Have felxible hours
  • Be prepared for physically demaning tasks if the need arises
  • Take and pass a Department of Transportation Drug Test
  • Comply with uniform guidelines
  • Preferably speak more than one language

Requirements

For Pilots

In order to be considered, you must:

  • Be atleast 21 years of age at the time of applying
  • Be eligoble to work in the U.S.
  • Hold an Air Transport Pilot certificate (ATP)
  • Have (atleast) a second class medical certificate
  • Take and pass a Department of Transportation Drug Test
  • Comply with uniform guidelines
  • Have previous flight experience
