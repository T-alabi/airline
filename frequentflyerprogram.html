FREQUENT FLYER PROGRAM

Join The Frequent Flyer Program

Join the frequent flyer program! With this exclusive feature of Ventus Airlines, you can earn miles
that can total up to a free trip! You can spend your miles any way you want. Any time! Any Day!

How are points awarded?

The following conditions must be met if you want to earn points from a flight purchase:

  • You must provide your Frequent Flyers ID number with purchase of flight ticket.
  • Every $8 spent on your ticket results in 5 free miles.
  • Every $10 spent on in-flight accommodations will get you 2 free miles.

Things to Note

  • Fractions of miles will not be awarded.
  • Miles will only be awarded to whomever the frequent flyer ID belongs to.
  • There is no transfer of miles allowed.
  • Miles and money are not interchangeable for in-flight accommodations.
  • When spending miles, you must put your frequent flyer ID number.
  • When spending miles, if amount is not specified, the required amount for the purchase will be withdrawn.
