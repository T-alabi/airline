SAFE PRACTICES

Ventus Airlines will continue to follow these safe practices until further notice.

Personal Protective Equipment

We encourage passengers and employees of Ventus Airlines to bring and use personal protective equipment. In the absence of masks or gloves, they will be distributed to passengers. This does not mean you can take an unlimited amount of the equiptment. Please stay safe by keeping your mask and gloves on at all times.


Social Distancing

To keep distance between our passengers, we will be limiting the seating on all flights until further notice. We highly encourage passengers to keep distance between themselves and others- this includes families.


Thank you!

Thank you for cooperating with our terms. We at Ventus Airlines will continue to support our customers, in the air or not. We want to thank all front line and medical workers, which is why all medical workers will be receiving a special discount until quarantine ends for all East Coast states. You are the real heroes; let us be the wind beneath your wings.

RECOMMENDED DESTINATIONS

These are the most popular destinations right now! Take a look and see what you like.

Washington, D.C.
As springtime approaches, Washington D.C. blooms with gentle, pink cherry blossoms. Throughout March and April, the National Cherry Blossom Festival holds special events and tours. Washington, D.C. belongs on the top of your bucket list! Book today!

Niagara Falls, NY
The vast, majestic scenery of cascading ultramarine waters cools the feverish intensity of working life. Featuring boat tours, guided hikes, historical talks, and more, Niagara Falls has activities for everyone. Book a ticket and enjoy breathtaking views! Book today!

Fallingwater - Mill Run, PA
Fallingwater, designed by Frank Lloyd Wright, is an ecological house built partially over a waterfall. Explore both the interior and the exterior of the house on a guided tour. As an architectural masterpiece, Fallingwater is breathtaking! Book today!
