About Us

THE WIND BENEATH YOUR WINGS

Established in 2020, Ventus Airlines is a discount airline based at Newark Liberty International Airport. Ventus Airlines primarily services the northeast and south-atlantic regions of the United States.

As “The Wind Beneath Your Wings”, we are committed to providing our passengers with enjoyable, comfortable experiences for low fares. We offer excellent prices and services to our passengers so that they can rely on us as their Wind. We aim to help travelers spread their Wings and fly to new destinations, new adventures, and new opportunities.

Mission

Traveling shouldn't break the bank. Booking airline tickets should be fast, easy, and cheap.

We believe everyone should be able to travel without breaking the bank. Traveling allows you to explore new places, meet new people, connect with family and friends, forge business opportunities, etc. When traveling, you can temporarily escape from the hectic, stressful pace of daily life. By traveling, you create unforgettable, lifetime memories. With low-cost ticket prices, we hope to enable more people to travel.
